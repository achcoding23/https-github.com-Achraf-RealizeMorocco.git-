Realize Your Move

Our Comprehensive Services

Every step of your journey, visualized and supported.

01

Orientation Visits

Before you relocate, experience Morocco through our personalized discovery trips. We plan guided visits to neighborhoods, schools, and everyday life spots so you can make an informed and confident decision. It’s the ideal first step to turn your vision into reality.

02

Home & Relocation Support

Finding a home in Morocco can be both exciting and overwhelming. Our relocation support helps you choose the right area, understand rental or purchase agreements, and handle all practical details from utilities to moving logistics. Whether you’re relocating to Tétouan, Tangier, or the coastal towns of the North, we make sure your transition feels effortless.

03

Legal & Administrative Guidance

Understanding Moroccan bureaucracy and paperwork can be a challenge. We help you manage it all. From opening a bank account to signing contracts and understanding taxes, we provide practical, reliable guidance. We also work closely with local professionals to ensure your move and daily life remain legally secure.

04

Residency & Visa Assistance

Applying for a long-stay visa or residency permit in Morocco can feel complicated. We make it simple. Our team guides you through every requirement, prepares your documentation, and connects you with the right local offices to ensure a smooth process. We assist with temporary, permanent, and family residency so you can focus on your new life with peace of mind.

05

Business Setup & Investment

For entrepreneurs and investors, Morocco offers opportunity and growth, if you know where to begin. We assist with company formation, legal registration, permits, and introductions to trusted local partners. Whether you’re starting a small business or expanding your operations, our local insight helps you navigate Moroccan regulations with confidence.

06

Local Integration & Lifestyle Support

Moving to Morocco isn't just about logistics, it's about belonging. We help you and your family adapt through language lessons, school guidance, and cultural introductions. You'll discover the rhythms of Moroccan life and connect with local and expat communities that make the North so special.

Your trusted partner for seamless relocation and investment in the beautiful country of Morocco. Making your dream a reality.
