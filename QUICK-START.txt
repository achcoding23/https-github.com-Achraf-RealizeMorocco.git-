## Test It Works

### On Your Phone:
1. Open your website
2. You should see a hamburger menu (☰) in the top right
3. Click it to open the menu
4. Everything should fit on screen (no sideways scrolling)

### On Desktop:
1. Open your website
2. You should see the normal horizontal menu
3. Everything looks like before but better!
